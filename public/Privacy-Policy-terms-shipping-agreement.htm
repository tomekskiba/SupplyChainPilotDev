Baxter Privacy Policy and Delivery Terms & Conditions

Introduction

Welcome to Baxter Healthcare Homecare Services program. For purposes of the Policy, references to "we," "our," and "us" mean Baxter.

Homecare Services provides a wide range of information to our patients, including product order management, product delivery and healthcare information. We collect information from you, including personally identifiable information (your full name, e-mail address and so on) and nonpersonally identifiable information.  This Privacy Policy ("Policy") is designed to tell you about our information collection practices– that is, the ways we collect information; what kinds of information we collect; why we collect information; how we use information we collect; with whom we share information we collect; how you can access, amend, and delete information we collect from you; and what kinds of security we use to protect information you provide. Information provided by Homecare Services is not meant to be a substitute for the advice provided by a physician or other medical professional. This information should not be used for diagnosing a health problem or disease; visitors should always consult their own physician.

 

Scope of Policy

Please be aware that this Policy applies only to Baxter’s Homecare Services program.

General Information

The Homecare Services order management and delivery services provide home delivery of dialysis supplies.  Personal information will be provided by you and your dialysis unit staff to facilitate the delivery and management of your supplies.  This information includes, but not limited to, your name, address, phone number and prescription information.  By participating in Baxter’s Homecare Services program and providing your personal information, you consent to its use by Baxter and third party consultants who are performing work on behalf of Baxter for the purposes described in this Policy. In some cases your information will be used to identify you as candidates for other patient programs. In these cases you will be contacted and given the opportunity to opt out of these programs.

 

Delivery Services

Baxter’s Homecare Services Representatives will work with you and your dialysis unit staff to set up your account and initial order and manage your ongoing home supply requirements.  Baxter’s Service Specialists will bring your supplies inside your home and rotate your supplies upon your request.  In some cases, an alternate delivery company, such as United Parcel Services (UPS) may deliver your product. 

If you or a responsible party are not home during your scheduled delivery, Baxter’s Service Specialist will contact your dialysis facility to ask for approval to leave the supplies in a safe place (covered porch, garage, etc.), deliver to a neighbor who can sign for the shipment, or bring the supplies back to the warehouse, requiring you to call Baxter’s Homecare Services to arrange for a redelivery.  The driver will leave a notice on your door indicating which action was taken.  If redelivery is required, your dialysis unit may incur additional costs.

Supply orders of 5 cases or less will be delivered by an alternate delivery company such as a commercial carrier or United Parcel Services (UPS).

Please be aware that the commercial carriers we use have been advised of the special needs of our patients.  However, many commercial carriers are not able to provide the same delivery services that Baxter’s Service Specialists typically provides to you (such as the pre-delivery call providing a specific delivery time window or delivering supplies to a desired location within your home).

Baxter may share your information with independent contractors or consultants who provide technical, administrative or market research support to Baxter. We reserve the right to sell, lease, transfer, or otherwise share without restriction aggregated, deidentified, and other nonpersonally identifiable information regarding demographics and other statistics about our programs. We reserve the right to use or disclose personal information provided via this program as necessary to respond to your requests, comply with applicable laws, regulations, legal process, and law enforcement inquiries, as required by litigation, to take precautions against liability, and protect the security and integrity of the program and the safety of its users.
